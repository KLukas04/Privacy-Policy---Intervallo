Privacy Policy - Intervallo

The Intervallo app does not collect, use, or back up user data. No data is sent to servers.
Only your own exercises, which you can create in the app, under the option "Exercises", are stored locally on your device.
Also stored locally is the length of the interval, the pause, the pause between the runs and the number of runs
. We do not have access to this data. Likewise, we do not request access to your photos, location, etc.
